Route to Climate Change¶

Route to Climate Change emphasizes the pressing impact of global warming and environmental degradation along athletic routes. It showcases how climate change affects familiar localities, making the global issue more tangible and urging immediate action.

  • Route: Users input their Strava activity, which sets the geographic parameters for our analysis.
  • Climate Impact: Leveraging scientific datasets, we gather data on temperature variations (Berkeley Earth), sea level changes, and air quality in proximity to the specified Strava activity.

An essential aspect of Route to Climate Change is the acknowledgment and recognition of indigenous territories, retrieved from Native Land.

User Instructions¶

  1. Strava Authorization:
  • Click on the provided link to authorize with Strava.
  • You will be redirected to Strava's login page. Log in using your Strava credentials.
  • After logging in, Strava will ask for permission to share your activity data. Grant the necessary permissions.
  • You will be redirected to a callback URL. *This website is broken (This site can’t be reached, localhost refused to connect). That's normal.* You just need to copy the entire URL from your browser's address bar.
  1. Enter the Callback URL:
  • Return to the *Route to Climate Change* tool.
  • In the "Callback" field, paste the copied URL.
  1. Enter Your Strava Activity URL:
  • Navigate to your desired activity on Strava's website.
  • Copy the URL of that activity from your browser's address bar.
  • Return to the *Route to Climate Change* tool and paste the activity URL in the provided field.
  1. Get climate data:
  • Click the "Get climate data" button.
  • The tool will analyze the area around your Strava activity and display climate data available.
  1. Explore the Results:
  • View the effects of climate change over time along your athletic route.

Disclaimer: The data presented in Route to Climate Change is not exhaustive. It encompasses data from a few sources with limited spatial and temporal resolution.
