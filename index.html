Pedro Pinheiro-Chagas

I am a postdoctoral fellow working with Stanislas Dehaene at NeuroSpin, in the outskirts of Paris.

My research focuses on understanding the architecture and dynamics of human high-level cognition, with a particular interest in elementary mathematics.

I use a combination of continuous behavioral measures (e.g. trajectory-tracking) and machine learning applied to time-resolved brain signals, such as MEG and iEEG.
